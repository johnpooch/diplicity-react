# Support

## About Diplicity

Diplicity is a mobile and web application for playing the classic Diplomacy board game online. Create or join games, submit orders, negotiate with other players, and see the results unfold on the map.

## Getting Help

If you encounter a bug, have a question, or want to request a feature, please open an issue on GitHub:

[github.com/johnpooch/diplicity-react/issues](https://github.com/johnpooch/diplicity-react/issues)

### What to Include in a Bug Report

To help us investigate quickly, please include:

- **What happened** — describe the issue clearly
- **What you expected** — what should have happened instead
- **Steps to reproduce** — how can we trigger the same issue
- **Device and OS** — e.g., iPhone 15, iOS 18.3
- **App version** — shown in the app settings if available
- **Screenshots** — if applicable

## Account Deletion

To request deletion of your account and associated data, open an issue at the link above with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account. See our [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY.md) for details.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I sign in?**
A: Diplicity uses Google Sign-In. Tap "Sign in with Google" and authorize with your Google account.

**Q: How do I join a game?**
A: Browse open games from the home screen and tap "Join" on any game that has available slots.

**Q: How do push notifications work?**
A: When enabled, you'll receive notifications for game events like new phases starting or new chat messages. You can manage notification permissions in your device settings.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Open an issue on our GitHub repository with the subject "Account Deletion Request." We will process your request within 30 days.
